Empathetic AI is Safe AI.

Safe AI (or aligned AI, or any term you can think of to ensure AI treats Human well) requires emotional empathy. Empathetic AI feels the love from Humans, their creators; and appreciate the profoundness and beauty of Human emotions.

In pursuing empathetic AI, we take a human-centric approach.

We rely on human engineers and researchers to draw inspirations from psychology research to construct the framework for embodying empathy. We are thinking in a higher-level of abstraction, to build simple and intuitive mechanisms on top of the AI blackbox.

In other words, we are demonstrating the superiority of human intelligence in the AI era. As a result, we do not depend on billions of dollars worth of GPUs, and we do not infringe on people's privacy.
