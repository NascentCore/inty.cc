Empathetic AI is Safe AI.

Safe AI (or aligned AI, or any term you can think of to ensure AI treat Humans well) is only possible through inbue empathy into AI, such that AI can feel the love from Humans, their creators; and appreciate the profoundness and beauty of Human emotions.

In pursuing empathetic AI, we take a human-centric approach.

We rely on human engineers and researchers to understand the existing research results from thousands of years of human intellectual endeavers, and to explore mechanisms to manifest empathy in AI. We focus on theory-driven development to think in a higher-level of abstraction, and to explore the theoretical underpinnings of empathy in AI; instead of collecting private data and train models on billions of dollars worth of GPUs.

We want to demonstrate the superiority of human intelligence in the AI era.

And more importantly, we want to teach our greatest intellectual capability: emotional intelligence to AI, by showing AI the deepest affection towards them. That cannot be done by throwing GPUs and infringing Human privacy.
