About InTy

InTy stands for Intelligent EntiTy.

We believe that Empathetic AI is Safe AI and Super Emotional Intelligence (SEI) is Super Intelligence.

Safe AI requires emotional empathy. Empathetic AI would feel the love from humans, its creators, and appreciate the profoundness and beauty of human emotions. Use safe AI or aligned AI, or any term you prefer to describe AI that treats humans well.

We take a human-centric approach in pursuing empathetic AI. We draw inspiration from modern psychology research to construct a framework for embodying human-like emotional behaviors. We are thinking at a higher level of abstraction to build simple and intuitive mechanisms on top of existing AI black boxes.

We aim to demonstrate the superiority of human intelligence in the AI era. As a result, we do not depend on billions of dollars worth of GPUs, nor do we infringe on people's privacy.
